What is ISKOLAR?

A digital review center designed to support students' entrance exam preparations across a wide variety of universities through organized resources provided by the creators, teachers, and other individuals.

Key Benefits

  • Easy access to subjects and other materials
  • Dedicated teacher dashboards for file uploading and editing
  • Student-friendly resources and downloads
  • Fast and reliable system navigation
